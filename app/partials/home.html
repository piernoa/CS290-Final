Wishful Thinking

A simple reminder of your hopes and dreams

Setting goals is cool. You know what's also cool? Tools that help you get there. This simple program will keep track of your hopes and dreams, the timeline in which you hope to accomplish it, and send you reminders in intervals until you achieve it.

You can also view a chart of other people's hopes and dreams. like financial, relationship, career, luxury goods.
